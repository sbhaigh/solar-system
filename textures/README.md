## Required Files (2K resolution recommended):

- `sun_2k.jpg` - Sun surface texture
- `mercury_2k.jpg` - Mercury surface
- `venus_2k.jpg` - Venus surface
- `earth_2k.jpg` - Earth surface texture
- `moon_2k.jpg` - Moon surface texture
- `mars_2k.jpg` - Mars surface texture
- `jupiter_2k.jpg` - Jupiter clouds
- `saturn_2k.jpg` - Saturn surface
- `uranus_2k.jpg` - Uranus surface
- `neptune_2k.jpg` - Neptune surface

## Image Format:

- JPG or PNG
- Square power-of-2 resolution (1024x512, 2048x1024, 4096x2048, etc.)
- Equirectangular projection (latitude/longitude mapped to x/y)
